<h2>Custom Data for Post</h2>
It appears that data entered into the name value pairs at the top of the _posts/mypost.textile 
file will be available in the posts namespace. This feature was used in these pages to allow
tags which the templates will use to tell the delicious feeds which tag to use to filter.
