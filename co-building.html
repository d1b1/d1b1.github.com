What is Co-Building?

The idea of co-building is relatively new. In the past, this type of support was called a technical co-founder or fractional co-founder. It's the same concept: having an experienced technical partner who helps you build faster and make better decisions—without the full-time commitment or equity stake of a traditional co-founder.
