What is a fCTO? (fractional CTO)

A fractional CTO (fCTO) provides interim tech leadership for growth-stage businesses when the CTO seat is empty or overloaded. I help teams stabilize delivery, align priorities, and drive measurable outcomes—without a rebuild.

Growth-stage businesses face consistent challenges: delivery stalls, priorities blur, and risk increases when leadership is missing. Every tech decision needs to balance risk reduction, delivery cadence, and business outcomes. That's where a fractional CTO comes in.

I work with your existing team and vendors to restore delivery governance, clarify priorities, and ship targeted improvements. My approach focuses on practical outcomes that work for where you are now—not theoretical solutions for where you might be in two years.

When do you need a fractional CTO?

Common triggers include: the CTO departed or is transitioning, delivery has become inconsistent, priorities aren't translating into outcomes, or a high-stakes initiative needs executive-level leadership. You need someone who can step in immediately and lead your existing team and vendors—without disrupting operations.

As a serial founder (Techstars 2018) who's worked with growth-stage businesses, I understand the challenges of leadership transitions and delivery gaps. I've led teams through transitions, and I know what works—and what doesn't—when tech impacts revenue or risk.
