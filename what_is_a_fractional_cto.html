What is a fCTO? (fractional CTO)

A fractional CTO (fCTO) is your on-demand technical co-founder—someone who helps founders solve problems quickly and practically when you can't find or afford a full-time technical co-founder.

Startups are always racing against the clock with limited funding, limited time, and a lean team. Every tech decision needs to drive ROI and get you to revenue positive faster. That's where a fractional CTO comes in.

I apply a "low effort to high-impact" filter to all decisions, assessments, and recommendations. This approach provides founders with the tools to balance limited resources and find stage-relevant solutions that work for where you are right now—not where you hope to be in two years.

When do you need a fractional CTO?

You seek out a fractional CTO when you can not find or afford a full-time, salaried and equity-motivated technical co-founder. When you need point solutions, an outside perspective, and options—without the full-time commitment.

As a serial founder (Techstars 2018), I know the pain of having to make decisions based on limited information, time, and resources. I've been in the trenches, and I know what works—and what doesn't—at different stages of a startup's journey.

I'm not just an advisor—I'm a coder at heart. When needed, I roll up my sleeves and dig in: configure cloud providers, debug CI/CD processes, upgrade libraries, integrate auth providers, and yes—write code.

Beyond the basics
