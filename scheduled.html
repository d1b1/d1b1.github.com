What Visibility do you need?

When working with me, you might want to think about how I can help, and what mode you want that help to take. You the CEO/Founder/King, where do you want me to be?

1. "Behind the Throne"

This is when I work directly with you, but am not visible to the public. This mode is valuable to founders or CEOs who need to own the outcome of a project and messaging to a larger audience.

Off LinkedIn. Stealth.
2. "In front of the Throne"

In this mode, my work is visible to everyone. This can be for fundraising purposes, sales, team or to show progress to investors.

On LinkedIn

What Responsiveness can you expect?

Different goals require different responsiveness.

1. Point Solutions

Billed hourly. 100% response to specific requests. Good for initial engagements. Strategy, without execution.

  • Level: By the hour
  • Responsiveness: 1-2 days
  • Prepaid Hour
2. Retainer

Longer term engagements, with a fixed price for a specific issue.

  • Level: Min of 20% of a month.
  • Responsiveness: 1 day
  • Prepaid Retainer
3. Package

When someone needs long term and deeper involvement, with autonomy and self direction.

  • Level: Min. of 20% of a month.
  • Responsiveness: 1-2 day
  • Prepaid Package

How to launch?

What is required to get started? After a intro meeting, upon request, I will provide the following documents.

1. Signed MSA

Standard Master Service Agreement to handle the legalities of the engagement; IP assignment, jurisdiction, mediation etc.

No red lines.

2. Signed SOW

These can change over time. Any change in role, expectation or deliverables should be signed off.

  • Includes Scope
  • Includes Pricing
  • Includes Timeline
  • Includes Payment Information
3. Signed NDA

I sign your NDA. If needed, I can provide my own NDA for us to use.

All payments must be made electronically via ACH provided in the SOW.
