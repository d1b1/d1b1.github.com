What Visibility do you need?

Consider how I can assist you and the role you envision for me. As the CEO or founder, where would you like me to fit in?

1. "Behind the Throne"

This is when I work directly with you, but am not visible to the public. This mode is valuable to founders or CEOs who need to own the outcome of a project and messaging to a larger audience.

Off LinkedIn. Stealth.
2. "In front of the Throne"

In this mode, I work directly with you and am visible to the public. This approach is beneficial for founders or CEOs who need their company or outside stakeholders to see and value my contributions.

On LinkedIn. Visible.

What Responsiveness can you expect?

Different goals require different responsiveness.

1. Point Solutions

Billed hourly. 100% directed to specific requests. You call the ball and set the agenda. Good for initial engagements. Strategy, without execution.

  • Level: By the hour
  • Responsiveness: 1-2 days
  • Prepaid Hour

2. Retainer

Long-term engagements with a fixed price for addressing specific issues.

  • Level: Min of 20% of a month.
  • Responsiveness: 1 day
  • Prepaid Retainer
3. Package

This package offers clients a deeper level of engagement and problem-solving, where they purchase a set number of months for comprehensive involvement.

  • Level: Min. of 20% of a month.
  • Responsiveness: 1-2 day
  • Prepaid Package

How to launch?

What is required to get started? After an introductory meeting, upon request, I will provide the following documents:

1. Signed MSA

The Master Service Agreement (MSA) outlines the legal framework for our engagement. It covers important aspects such as intellectual property assignment, jurisdiction, and mediation processes.

No red lines.

2. Signed SOW

The Statement of Work (SOW) outlines the specific details of our engagement; scope, pricing, timeline, and payment information. When a project needs change, the SOW is updated and signed off.

  • Includes Scope
  • Includes Pricing
  • Includes Timeline
  • Includes Payment Information
3. Signed NDA

I will sign your company's NDA.

Alternatively, I can provide my own NDA for our use if necessary.

All payments should be made electronically via ACH, as specified in the Statement of Work (SOW).
