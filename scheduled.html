Consider how I can assist you and the role you envision for interim tech leadership. As the CEO or executive team, where would you like me to fit in?

1. "Behind the Throne"

I work directly with you and your team, but am not visible to the public. This mode is valuable to CEOs who need to own the outcome and messaging to stakeholders, board members, or customers.

Behind the scenes. Stealth.
2. "In front of the Throne"

In this mode, I work directly with you and am visible to the public. This approach is beneficial for CEOs who need their company or stakeholders to see and value interim tech leadership.

Public-facing. Visible.

I work with growth-stage SMBs through packaged engagements—not hourly billing. This ensures predictable costs and focused outcomes.

1. Diagnostic

Fixed-scope assessment to identify constraints, priorities, and delivery gaps. You get clear recommendations and a roadmap—no hourly billing.

  • Duration: 2-3 weeks
  • Deliverable: Assessment & roadmap
  • Pricing: Fixed package
2. Delivery Sprint

Fixed-scope sprint to deliver targeted wins: restore delivery cadence, optimize costs, or address specific tech challenges. Works with your existing team.

  • Duration: 4-8 weeks
  • Deliverable: Measurable outcomes
  • Pricing: Fixed package
3. Interim CTO

Ongoing interim tech leadership to maintain delivery cadence, manage risk, and ensure priorities stay aligned. Bridges the gap until you hire a full-time CTO or no longer need the coverage.

  • Duration: 3-6 months
  • Deliverable: Sustained cadence
  • Pricing: Monthly package

Here's how we'll structure our engagement:

1. Letter of Intent

I'll send you a Letter of Intent (LOI) to outline our initial understanding and scope. This sets clear expectations for the engagement and deliverables.

2. SOW & MSA

We'll formalize with a Statement of Work (SOW) outlining scope, pricing, timeline, and payment, plus a Master Service Agreement (MSA) for the legal framework.

No red lines. Standard terms, fast execution.

3. NDA

I will sign your company's NDA to protect your confidential information.

Alternatively, I can provide my own NDA for our use if necessary.

All payments should be made electronically via ACH, as specified in the Statement of Work (SOW).
