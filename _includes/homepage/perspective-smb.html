My Perspective

Growth-stage businesses face consistent patterns: delivery stalls, priorities blur, and risk increases when leadership is missing. Here's how I approach common challenges when tech impacts revenue or risk: Learn more.

  Observed   Solution
Context Priorities get lost when the CTO seat is empty or overloaded. → Interim leadership restores delivery cadence through clear frameworks and priority alignment, bridging the gap when tech challenges impact revenue.
Delivery Teams have capacity but delivery is inconsistent. Vendors are expensive but results are unclear. → Lead existing teams and vendors with governance, clear cadence, and measurable outcomes without a rebuild.
Risk Security, reliability, and compliance concerns grow as the business scales. → De-risk systematically: identify constraints, prioritize fixes, and establish clear ownership without slowing the business.
Growth Teams can be siloed into a specific approach or tech stack, limiting scalability. → An outside perspective helps leadership rethink tech stack and processes, identifying constraints and modernizing pragmatically.
